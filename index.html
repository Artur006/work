The concept of “man” has a deep meaning, its meaning can be viewed from different sides. From the point of view of biology, this is just one of the types of life on the planet. From the position of sociology, a person is much more, he is a product of his time, culture, upbringing. In psychology, he is a carrier of qualities, abilities, talents, emotions and desires that are peculiar only to him.
